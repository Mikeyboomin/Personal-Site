About me


Hey, I'm Michael! I'm a software developer working in Nigeria. Welcome to my digital space where I share my passions, projects, tutorials and ideas.

I'm currently working on some tutorials and articles that are going to help people who are interested in learning how to code but don't know how to get started. I'll break everything down into simple to understand concepts that even a 5 year old can grasp and what's exciting is that it's gonna be all free!. I'm gonna focus on topics like HTML, CSS, JavaScript and the frameworks associated with it, i.e React and Gatsby.

My site has no ads, no affiliate links, no tracking or analytics, no sponsored posts, and no paywall. My only motivation for this site is to share what I've learned with the world and document notes for myself, and hopefully connect with a few people.

You can contact me by email at info.mikeyboomin@gmail.com to say hi! I always appreciate meeting new people.
